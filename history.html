HISTORY OF PERYATIM

The history of the Sarawak Orphans Welfare Board (PERYATIM) began when the Sarawak State Orphans Welfare Organization (PKAYNS) was registered on 3 May 1978 under the Societies Act. The name was changed in 2005 and was replaced by the establishment of a charitable trust organization called the Sarawak Orphans Welfare Board under The Charitable Trusts Ordinance, Cap. 1994 came into force on 1 July 2005, or better known as PERYATIM. The first patron of PERYATIM is the late Datuk Amar Puan Sri Hajjah Laila Taib with the main purpose of establishing this complex is to look after the welfare of orphans throughout the State of Sarawak.

The historical chronology of the opening of four (4) PERYATIM Branch Complexes in Sarawak is as follows: -

  1. Laila Taib Welfare Complex (KKLT), Kuching - Inaugurated on 24 June 1993

  2. Hamidah Yakup Welfare Complex (KKHY), Miri - Inaugurated on 28 May 2008

  3. Sibu Orphan Welfare Complex - Started operations in June 2014

  4. Bintulu Orphan Welfare Complex - Started operation in March 2015

All these efforts are not only the result of the hard work of PERYATIM citizens alone, but more as a result of the contributions and caring attitude of the people who always contribute donations to PERYATIM.
